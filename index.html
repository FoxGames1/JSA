About us

At JOKS SOCCER ACADEMY AND GLOBAL SOLUTIONS, we are passionate about developing grassroots football talent and empowering the next generation of players and coaches. Our mission is to create world-class football academies and offer innovative vocational training foraspiring coaches, ensuring they are equipped with cutting-edge techniques to nurture young footballers.

We also provide international internships, offering players a chance to gain global experience, alongside a dedicated department for footballers’ representation, helping talented athletes connect with top-tier clubs worldwide. With strong partnerships across Europe, including Spain, Poland, Estonia, Lithuania and Latvia, we offer unique international opportunities for players to showcase their skills.

Our main office is based in Nigeria, with a growing international presence, and we are committed to providing a pathway to success for aspiring footballers and coaches on a global scale
